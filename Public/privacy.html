Privacy Policy

At PingPop, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect and how we use, protect, and share that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic account details such as your username, email address, and profile data. We do not store your passwords in plain text. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted and are never accessible by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to authenticate your identity, provide access to our messaging platform, and enhance your experience. We never sell or share your data.

3. Security Measures

We apply industry-standard encryption techniques and security best practices to ensure your data is safe from unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You may access or delete your account at any time. If you need assistance, please email us at support@pingpop.com.

5. Updates

If we make changes to this policy, we will notify you through the app or via email.
